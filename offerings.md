      <h1 style="color: #E6D5B8;">Our Offerings</h1>

    <p>
      Every dish at Namcheon Maru begins with the same question — what does this ingredient want to become?
      No shortcuts, no compromise. Just the honest flavors of Jeonju, served with care.
    </p>

    <hr class="major" />

    <h2 style="color: #E6D5B8;">🍚 The Golden Bibimbap (간장비빔밥) — ₩13,000</h2>
    <p>
      Our signature dish. Soy Sauce Bibimbap replaces the conventional gochujang with a deeply savory,
      house-aged golden soy sauce — crafted from a recipe passed down through generations.
      Fresh mountain herbs, seasonal vegetables, premium rice. Pure umami, no heat.
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Minimum order:</strong> 2 persons. If other guests are already ordering, 1 person is welcome — please inquire.</li>
      <li><strong>Vegetarian option:</strong> Available upon request.</li>
    </ul>

    <hr class="major" />

    <h2 style="color: #E6D5B8;">🍜 Red Bean Noodle Soup (팥칼국수) — ₩10,000</h2>
    <p>
      Hand-cut wheat noodles simmered in our signature red bean broth.
      Subtly sweet, deeply savory, wholly plant-based.
      A uniquely Jeonju experience — warm, nourishing, unlike anything you've tried before.
    </p>

    <hr class="major" />

    <h2 style="color: #E6D5B8;">🍜 Clam Noodle Soup (칼국수) — ₩10,000</h2>
    <p>
      Hand-cut noodles in a clean, briny clam broth — light yet deeply satisfying.
      The kind of bowl that tastes like the sea on a quiet afternoon.
    </p>

    <hr class="major" />

    <h2 style="color: #E6D5B8;">🥣 Red Bean Porridge (팥죽) — ₩12,000</h2>
    <p>
      Slow-simmered red beans blended into a smooth, velvety porridge with soft rice balls (새알심).
      Naturally sweet, warming, entirely plant-based.
      A bowl that feels like a quiet moment of stillness.
    </p>

    <hr class="major" />

    <h2 style="color: #E6D5B8;">🍣 Chilled Buckwheat Soba (냉소바) — ₩10,000</h2>
    <p>
      Escape the heat. Chilled soba noodles bathed in an icy, deeply savory broth —
      a revitalizing bowl that arrives cool and leaves you refreshed.
      Available seasonally.
    </p>

    <hr class="major" />

    <h2 style="color: #E6D5B8;">🥟 Handmade Dumplings (만두) — ₩8,000</h2>
    <p>
      Small bites, considered flavor. Each dumpling is folded by hand and packed with
      a savory blend of premium ingredients — a satisfying burst in every bite.
      Served steamed or pan-fried upon request.
    </p>
